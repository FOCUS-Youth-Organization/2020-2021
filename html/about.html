About




Our Story

FOCUS Youth is a registered non-profit organization founded in February 2020 by two high school students from St. Robert Catholic High School. Currently, with a team of over 30 motivated and passionate individuals, we host a variety of events and initiatives aligned with our core values.


Our organization was founded on the principle that youth have the potential to change the world. We strive to provide them with the tools necessary to succeed and create a difference as many may be interested in helping their community but are unsure where to start. Through our initiatives we aspire to help you stay informed about various issues around the world and prompt them to take action.

Our Mission

We recognize the potential that youth have to change the world and create a positive impact. Our organization was created with the intention of educating, inspiring, and motivating youth. This goal is achieved through events, initiatives, and posts that provide youth with opportunities to take action. Without one singular focus, we strive to improve every aspect of the world to the best of our ability.




Our Vision

FOCUS Youth strives to make a positive difference in the world, while also inspiring other youth to follow in our footsteps. We hope to instigate change through demonstrating the importance of giving back to the community and providing youth with the resources they need to reach their potential.
