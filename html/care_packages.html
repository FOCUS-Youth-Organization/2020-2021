Care Packages

Introducing FOCUS Youth’s newest initiative: Care Packages! In recognizing the challenges that the homeless face, especially during the pandemic, we will be delivering care packages containing survival essentials as well as COVID-19 supplies.

Care Package Contents

Each of our care packages are well thought through and contain vital supplies for the homeless to fight the bitter winter and COVID-19.
Contents of our care packages include...
  • Blankets
  • Hand sanitizers
  • Face masks
  • Gloves
  • Socks
  • Feminine products

Sponsor

This project is funded by the Rising Youth Grant! Please check out their website for more information about their community!
We thank Taking it Global, Rising Youth, the Canada Service Corps, and the government of Canada for funding this project.

Volunteer Opportunity!


We are currently looking for cards to put inside our care packages! The requirements are create a digital single-sided card (preferably Canva) and write any kind message of your choice for the homeless. Please do not include any personal information. Please email us your card, along with your full name and the time it took to create the card. For more details, send us a dm or an email to info@focusyouth.ca! Check out below for more information.
Update: We are no longer accepting card submissions. Thank you to everyone who submitted a card.

Want to help?

Thank you for your interest in helping out with our Care Packages initiative! We look forward to seeing the amazing cards you create! Below you will find everything you need to know regarding how to submit a card. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us on Instagram (@focusyouthorg) or email us at info@focusyouth.ca. Cards are due Friday, February 19th, at 11:59pm.

Card Requirements

    Include a nice message to a homeless person. Graphics and other designs are permitted.
  • Cards can be in color or black and white.
  • Cards must be a digital format (PDF or Canva link).
  • Maximum card size of 8.5 by 11.5 inches (letter size).
  • Cards must be single-sided. However, they can be folded in half to contain the message on the inside.
  • Do not include any personal information (name, address, etc).

Obtaining Volunteer Hours

  • There is a maximum of 5 cards per person, at a maximum of 3 volunteer hours per card. However, hours are given at the discretion of the FOCUS Youth Team.
  • Send the cards to info@focusyouth.ca. The cards should be either a Canva link or PDF.
  • Title the email “Care Package Cards”
  • In the email, include your full name, your grade, and the school you are currently attending.
  • Indicate how many hours were spent on each card. We will send you a proof of volunteering letter once we have received your email. If you would like us to sign a specific volunteer form, please include the form in the email.

Thank you for your interest and have fun!
