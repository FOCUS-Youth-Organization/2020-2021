Care Packages

Starting in 2020, FOCUS Youth Organization will be delivering care packages to the homeless every year. Our care packages are catered to the need of the homeless, including vital supplies to stay warm in the harsh winter weather. We hope that through our initiative, we inspire others to join us in making and delivering care packages, or finding other ways to help the homeless. Read more about this initiative here.
